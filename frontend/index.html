AI-Supported SME E-Commerce Management System

A modern and responsive business web application designed for women entrepreneurs and small business owners to manage products, orders, inventory, suppliers, and daily business operations through a database-connected management dashboard.

Go to Dashboard

System Features

Product Management

Add, view, update, and delete product information such as category, price, stock quantity, and critical stock level.

Order Tracking

Track customer orders, order status, quantities, total prices, shipping carrier, and estimated delivery dates.

Inventory Control

Monitor stock levels, identify products below the critical stock level, and get AI-based restocking suggestions.

Supplier Management

View and manage suppliers, track linked products, and keep supplier contact information organized.

AI Assistant

Ask questions about products, orders, inventory, suppliers, stock suggestions, and daily business summaries.
